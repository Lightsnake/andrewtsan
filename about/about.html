From Zero to...Self-Taught

Outside of a few programming courses in college, I've never had much real experience with coding. Once I've graduated from I wanted to pursue coding as a career and I've grown to love it.

I've always liked having a visual representation of my work so I felt like front-end development was great introduction into the coding world.

Everything that I've learned has been self-taught through various sources on the internet.

Outside of Coding

When I'm not coding I'm enjoying my time with video games and video making. I record vlogs for my youtube channel that document my life.

I've always grown up playing video games and it's one of the many ways I can connect with friends that are from either a few towns, states, or countries away from me .

My Future

My future is to one day be not just a successful front-end developer but to be a great developer.

To me, being a great developer is always strive to learn and to not give up when solving problems.

These are traits to me are what makes a great developer
